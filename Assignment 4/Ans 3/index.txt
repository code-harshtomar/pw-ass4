Accessibility in web development:Accessibility in web development refers to designing and coding websites in a way that ensures people with disability can perceive, understand, navigate and interact with digital content effectively.It involves making web content inclusive and usable for everyone
Impotance of Accessible Websites:
Inclusivity:Accessible website ensure that everyone,including people with disabilies, can access and use online information and services.
Legal Compliance: Many countries have laws requiring websites to be accessible, avoiding legal issues and promoting equal
User Experience: Accessible design often leads to improves user experiences for everyoneBusiness Growth: Accessible websites reach a wider audience, increasing engagment,loyality and potentail customer base.
Benifits for User Groups:
Visual Impairments:Accessibility provides screen reader support and text alternatives,aiding blind and visually impaired users.
Hearing Impairment: caption and transcripts benefit those with hearing impairments by making audio content understandable.
Motor Disabilities:Keyboard navigation and easy to click elements assist users with motor limitations.
cognitive disabilities:Accessible design simplifies content and layout aiding individuals with cognitive challenges.
Eldery Users; Accessible websites accommodate age related limitations ensuring usability for elder users.
Mobile users: Accessible design improves mobile experience through simplifies and responsive layouts.
